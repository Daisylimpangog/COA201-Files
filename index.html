Mission

As the country's Supreme Audit Institutions, the Commission on Audit is vested by the Constitutions with: the power and duty to audit and settle accounts pertaining to revenues and receipts, as well as expeditures or uses of funds and property the owned or held in trust by the goverment; exclusive autority to prescribe goverment accounting and auditing rules and regulations; responsibility to recommend measures necessary to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of goverment operations.

Vision

Promotion of good governance with the attributes of honesty, transparency, accountability, credibility, efficiency and effectiveness to enhance trust in the and foster a better quality of life for the Filipino people.
